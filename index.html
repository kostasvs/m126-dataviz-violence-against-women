How are crimes distributed across age and race?

While assaults and homicides peak in the age range of 20 - 40 years, sex offenses are highest in the age range of 10 - 25 years.

Assaults and homicides against Black or African American women seem to be skewed towards a younger age range than other races.

Homicides show a wider age spread than other crimes.
